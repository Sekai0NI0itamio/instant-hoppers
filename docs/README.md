# Instant-Hoppers

This mod makes Minecraft hoppers move items faster and pull items from the ground automatically.

## What It Does
- Hoppers transfer items instantly, moving full stacks in one go instead of one item at a time.
- They also suck up items that are lying on the ground right next to them.
- The transfer keeps going until the hopper is empty or the destination is full, so you don’t have to wait for multiple ticks.
- No extra buttons or recipes are needed; the change works automatically in any world.
- Hoppers can move full stacks in one go instead of only one item at a time.
- Hoppers also pull dropped items on top into themselves right away.

## Quick Facts



- Summary: Hate that hoppers move items one by one? Well this mod makes hoppers move full stacks in one go.

- Loader: `forge`

- Supported Minecraft versions: `1.12.2`
