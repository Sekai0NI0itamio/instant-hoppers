# Instant-Hoppers

A simple mod that makes hoppers move items faster and pull items from the ground automatically.

## What It Does
- Hoppers can transfer a full stack of items in one go instead of just one item at a time.
- They also suck up items that are lying on the ground right next to them.
- After moving one item, they keep moving more items until they can't move any more, all without any player input.
- No new blocks, items, or recipes are added; it just changes how vanilla hoppers work.
- Hoppers can move full stacks in one go instead of only one item at a time.
- Hoppers also pull dropped items on top into themselves right away.

## Quick Facts



- Summary: Hate that hoppers move items one by one? Well this mod makes hoppers move full stacks in one go.

- Loader: `forge`

- Supported Minecraft versions: `1.12.2`
